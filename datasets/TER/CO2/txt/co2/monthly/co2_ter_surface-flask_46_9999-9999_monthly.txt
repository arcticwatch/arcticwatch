# dataset_description : Carbon dioxide (CO2) in ambient and standard air samples is detected using a non-dispersive infrared (NDIR) analyzer. The measurement of CO2 in air is made relative to standards whose CO2 mole fraction is determined with high precision and accuracy. Because detector response is non-linear in the range of atmospheric  levels, at least 3 reference standards are used to calibrate detector response. Measurements are traceable to the WMO CO2 mole fraction scale by using  standards from NOAA, AES, Stockholm University and from2010 directly WMO CCL standards.The samples are collected into two evacuated flasks simultaneously. Stainless steel flasks with electropolished inner walls of 1.5l volume are used.
